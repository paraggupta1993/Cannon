"Cannon"

objective: To hit the cloud with our black balls, and save the cannon from clouds 'purple' balls.

Controls : 

z = zoomin
x = zoomout
c = switch camera between cannon or the last shooted ball.
r = rotate cannon anticlockwise
t = rotate cannon clockwisr
d = increase shooting power
s = shoot 
j = move cannon left
l = move cannon right
i = jump the cannon 

Scoring :

+10 : hitting the cloud ( level increases )
+1 : hitting the purple ball with Radius lower than the radius of black ball.
-1 : hitting the purple ball with Radius greater than the radius of black ball.

Level Increment : 

1. Level increases whenever we hit the cloud.
2. The number of purple balls increases on stage.
